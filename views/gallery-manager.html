The pictures in the scroller below are the pictures in your gallery

These pictures are in the same sequence as which they appear in your gallery. You can make changes to your gallery:

  1. by adding a new picture before / after an existing picture
  2. by removing an existing picture from the gallery
  3. by moving an picture in the gallery either to the left or to the right

To make any changes to the to a picture, click on the picture in the scroller below

IMPORTANT: There is currently an issue with the Add feature. If you add a picture to your gallery, the gallery is not immediately refreshed to show your changes. You need to click on any picture in the gallery to refresh it.
